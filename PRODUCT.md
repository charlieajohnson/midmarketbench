# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

MidMarketBench serves investment professionals testing whether frontier models can support lower-mid-market B2B software decisions, technical evaluators inspecting benchmark quality, and portfolio reviewers assessing the rigour of the underlying system. They need to understand the ranking quickly, then trace every claim to a case, route, score and cost artefact.

## Product Purpose

Publish a credible, live benchmark of workflow usefulness rather than general intelligence. Success means a reader can identify the leading observed models, understand the limits of a small run, and audit how each result was produced without sacrificing visual clarity or pace.

## Brand Personality

Institutional, vivid, exacting. The product should feel like a modern research institution with a distinct visual signature: calm in its claims, energetic in its identity, and unusually transparent about evidence.

## Anti-references

- Humanist Compute Atelier or Old Master painterly scenes.
- Generic AI SaaS pages with soft blue gradients, floating glass cards or vague claims.
- Beige luxury-editorial styling that uses ornament or serif italics as a proxy for authority.
- Dark cyber-terminal aesthetics, neon grids or dashboard cosplay.
- Rainbow gradients. The chromatic system is a controlled red, coral, orange and yellow spectrum.
- Italics in headlines or descriptive copy.
- Repeated uppercase microtype used as decoration rather than useful metadata.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence leads. Rankings, provenance and limitations remain more prominent than decorative treatment.
2. Colour creates recognition, not hype. Saturated fields form the identity while data surfaces stay calm and legible.
3. Structure signals authority. Fine rules, aligned columns and disciplined spacing make the benchmark feel inspectable.
4. One composition carries each viewport. Use a single dominant geometric field rather than accumulating effects.
5. Elegance comes from proportion. Roman typography, generous measure and precise alignment replace italics and ornament.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA across core journeys. Do not rely on colour alone for rank, status or score meaning. Preserve keyboard focus, semantic tables, mobile navigation, text resizing and a complete reduced-motion state. Saturated artwork may carry text only where contrast is explicitly proven.
